Foldable computer mouse that use a simple balloon as the main body frame. Exploring the boundaries between a functional product and a modern art piece.

Instructions

Simply inflate the balloon, tie it off, open the arch and buttons of the mouse, and insert the balloon under the arch, ensuring the knot fits into the designated hole.
